Ponies

Ponies are small but strong horses, beloved for their gentle nature and versatility.

  • Ponies stand under 14.2 hands tall.
  • They are known for friendly personalities.
  • Common breeds include Shetland, Welsh, and Connemara.
  • Ponies are often used in children's riding programs.
