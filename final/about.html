About New & Improv.'d

The oldest and boldest comedy group at JMU!

Our Mission

To provide an inclusive space where students can explore and enjoy comedy, build confidence, and entertain the JMU community.

About Us

New & Improv.'d is James Madison University's first improvisational comedy troupe. Founded in 1999, we have been successfully bringing laughs to the JMU campus ever since! We perform shows on campus, as well as off-campus venues in the greater Harrisonburg area. New & Improv.'d has performed with troupes from other universities, at charity events such as Relay for Life, and has traveled to both North Carolina and Washington D.C for comedy arts festivals. Our troupe also teams up with X2 comedy to perform family-fun comedy shows at local bars and breweries. New & Improv.'d has also partnered with JMU's College of Science and Mathematics to put on the science podcast, Demystifying the Expert.
